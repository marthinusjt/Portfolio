ABOUT ME

Hey there, my name is Marthinus Taljaard. I was born in South Africa and moved to the United States in 1998.

After graduating High School, I went to college for a year. Not enjoying what I was studying, decided to enlist in the United States Air Force where I served for five and a half years on active duty and was trained to be a medical laboratory technician. Afterwards, I went back to college for an aviation management degree.

After college, I went back to what I was comfortable with and found a job as a medical laboratory technician. I wanted to spend more time with my family but was unable to do so with my current job due to working third shift and decided that I needed to step out of my comfort zone and apply myself. With this, I enrolled at Eleven Fifty Academy to study JavaScript.
